90s Hip Hop

The 1990s are widely regarded as the golden age of hip hop. The decade saw the genre explode from regional scenes into a dominant force in popular music, driven by sharp lyricism, innovative sampling, and a rivalry between the East and West coasts that defined an era.

The Artists

The decade was shaped by a deep roster of artists across both coasts and beyond. A few of the most influential:

  • The Notorious B.I.G.
  • Tupac Shakur
  • Nas
  • Wu-Tang Clan
  • Dr. Dre
  • Snoop Dogg
  • A Tribe Called Quest
  • Outkast
